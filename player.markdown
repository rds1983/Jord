## Keyboard Commands

|Keys|Description|
|**Arrow Keys/Numpad**|Movement|
|**Numpad 5**|Wait Turn|
|**C**|Character|
|**I**|Equipment/Inventory|
|**E**|Use(i.e. pick items, descend a staircase, etc)|
|**A**|Abilities|

## City Buildings
|Symbol|Name|Description|
|B|Smithy|Weapons and armor shop
|G|Guild|Place where you can make a level
